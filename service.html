FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Rashtriya Gram Swaraj Abhiyan (RGSA) is a centrally sponsored scheme launched by the Ministry of Panchayati Raj to strengthen Panchayati Raj Institutions through capacity building and training programs. Kaivalyam is a recognized partner for conducting training and exposure visits under this program.

Training program duration varies based on the specific requirements and objectives. Typically, our programs range from 3-day intensive workshops to 5-day comprehensive training modules. Exposure visits usually last 5-7 days including travel and site visits.
