Our Journey

Timeline of Growth

2003 - Foundation

Kaivalyam Foundation was established with a vision to transform lives through education and social development.

2008 - Expansion

Expanded our reach to 10 cities, impacting over 50,000 lives through various education and healthcare programs.

2015 - Recognition

Received national recognition for our innovative approach to community development and sustainable solutions.

2020 - Digital Revolution

Launched digital education platforms and online healthcare services, reaching remote communities nationwide.

2024 - Global Impact

Achieved milestone of impacting 5 million lives with partnerships across multiple countries.

2025 - Future Vision

Committed to reaching 10 million lives by 2030 through innovative programs and sustainable initiatives.
